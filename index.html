About Me

I'm a computer scientist with expertise in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Sensor Fusion. I am finishing a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and have developed competitive 2D and 3D Object Detection models. I'm eager to contribute my skills and knowledge to new challenges.
