About Me

I'm a computer scientist with expertise in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Sensor Fusion. I am finishing a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and have developed competitive 2D and 3D Object Detection models. My projects include implementing Image Processing and Deep Learning models for perception in autonomous vehicles, as well as integrating GPS, IMU, and odometry for global location estimation. I'm eager to contribute my skills and knowledge to new challenges.
